    • Who am I?

    Not just an existential crisis!

    I'm a front end web developer based out of central California with a passion for Javascript. I primarily specialize in React but I'm always learning new things for practical and personal applications.

    Who have I worked with?

    I've worked primarily with non-profit organizations.


    My first work was with a small (now defunct) organization called the Skybrook Foundation The foundation primarily focused on spreading awareness for a disease called Sarcoidosis. My duties primarily boiled down to writing the entire web platform from scratch. I primarily did it as a favor to a friend of mine and as a means of training my skillset the best I could.



    The next place I worked was on a site called Vuee.io. This ultimately didn't work out because the work environment was entirely unprofessional and the platform was unpolished.


    The most recent organization I worked with I had to leave due to large family troubles: Open Learning Exchange. This organization (if you haven't heard of it) primarily makes a fantastic open source education system for use in impoverished countries. My duties mainly boiled down to catching and fixing bugs, however due to the previously mentioned family issues I didn't accomplish much before I had to leave.
